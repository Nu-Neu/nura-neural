## Methodology Notes

**Persona Score (0-100):** Composite estimate based on:
- **Factuality:** History of verified vs. false/misleading claims
- **Consistency:** Political U-turns, contradictions, or steady positions
- **Transparency:** Disclosure of affiliations and funding sources
- Higher scores indicate greater overall credibility and consistency

**Rhetoric Risk (Low/Medium/High):**
- **Low:** No documented hate speech or incitement to violence
- **Medium:** Some inflammatory rhetoric or alignment with aggressive policies
- **High:** Documented hate speech, incitement to violence, or terrorism designations
