# Product Discovery & Market Fit Summary

## 1. Vision
**Nura Neural** is an AI-powered intelligence platform designed to combat misinformation and analyze narratives within the Iranian information space. It moves beyond simple news aggregation to provide **Trust Scoring**, **Narrative Clustering**, and **Provenance Tracking**.

## 2. Target Audience (Personas)
Based on Belbin & Expert analysis of user needs:

| Persona | Role | Pain Point | Solution |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Sarah (The Activist)** | Diaspora Advocate | Needs verified evidence to counter state propaganda. | **Evidence Bank**: Quick access to verified sources and claims. |
| **Reza (The Journalist)** | Investigative Reporter | Overwhelmed by volume of news/fakes. | **Trust Scores**: AI-filtered feed highlighting high-credibility items. |
| **Global Observer** | Policy Maker / NGO | Lacks context on Farsi-language nuances. | **Narrative Clustering**: Grouping localized events into broader English narratives. |

## 3. Core Research Insights (Summary)
*Derived from IMTT Framework and Market Research.*

*   **Trust > Speed**: Users prefer delayed, verified news over instant rumors.
*   **The "State Media" Problem**: Official sources cannot be ignored but must be capped/weighted (Legacy ADR-005).
*   **Narrative Continuity**: Events are rarely isolated; they belong to long-running narrative arcs (e.g., "Woman, Life, Freedom").
